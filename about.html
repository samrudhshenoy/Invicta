About Us

Why We Created Invicta.

The Bay Area, home to Silicon Valley, the innovation capital of the world, is filled with ambitious millennials who are driven to rise in the ranks, create the next billion dollar company, and carve their place in society. However, many of those who fit this demographic, burdened by student debt, loans to pay back, and simply originating from a lower income background, often lack the financial resources to get started in such industries, especially when it comes to their look.

Appearance is always the first aspect one is judged by, and great appearances create great first impressions. Formal clothing is not commonly used among our demographic, but when it is used, it's for an important reason. That’s why we created Invicta: to provide a quick and easy rental service for customers to rent out clothes for a low price, and return them as soon as they're done. This way, we provide today’s younger and not-yet financially stable crowd with the apparel and means to succeed and make major developments in their life, by putting their best foot forward and presenting themselves in the best manner possible.
